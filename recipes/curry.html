Panang Curry with Fish

Malay/Thai Cuisine

Panang curry with chicken represents the diversity of Thailand's southern region. Panang refers to the island of Penang in Northern Malaysia bordering southern Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons Panang curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 4 fillets Tilapia
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce, or to taste
  • 6 makrut lime leaves, torn in half
  • Â¼ cup fresh Thai basil leaves

Steps

  1. Fry curry paste in oil over medium heat until fragrant.
  2. Stir in coconut milk and bring to a boil. Add fish.
  3. Stir fish sauce and lime leaves into mixture.
  4. Garnish with sliced red chile peppers and Thai basil leaves.
